How it works

This project provides a curated collection of Jupyter notebooks related to black hole perturbation theory. The notebooks contain mathematical models and simulations that explore various aspects of black hole dynamics under perturbations, including the effects of gravitational waves.

  1. Browse the library of Jupyter notebooks that have been pre-uploaded and made publicly available.
  2. Each notebook contains simulations and theoretical models relevant to the study of black hole perturbations.
  3. Explore the embedded outputs and interactive visualizations to gain insights into black hole behavior and gravitational wave interactions.
